About Me

Technical Excellence in Every Detail

With over 7 years of experience in technical drafting and design, I specialize in creating precise, industry-standard CAD drawings for architectural, mechanical, and electrical projects. My attention to detail ensures every dimension, annotation, and line meets professional standards.

I work with architects, engineers, and contractors to transform concepts into construction-ready documentation. From 2D floor plans to complex 3D models, I deliver accurate drawings that bring projects to life.
